Welcome to Our Pet Safety Adventure!

The Outdoor Cat Tracker is a smart, lightweight wearable tag designed for adventurous cats. It tracks location in real-time using GPS outdoors and BLE or Wi-Fi indoors, monitors activity levels, and sends geofencing alerts to keep your furry friend safe.

About Us

We are a team of four Computer Engineering students at Toronto Metropolitan University (TMU), passionate about combining technology with pet care. Inspired by the real-world challenge of ensuring the safety of outdoor cats, we’re motivated to create innovative solutions that bring peace of mind to pet owners.

Faculty Lab Coordinator: Meranda Salem

Student A

Focused on sensor selection, circuit/PCB design, and power management. Inspired by sustainable tech to keep devices lightweight.

Student B

Student C

Student D

Project Specifications

Key Features

  • GPS + BLE/Wi-Fi Hybrid Tracking
  • Low-Power Design (≤30g weight)
  • Activity Monitoring (Resting, Walking, Running)
  • Geofencing Alerts
  • E-Ink Display for Owner Contact
  • Mobile App Integration

Tech Stack

ESP32 Microcontroller, GPS Module, Accelerometer, Rechargeable Battery, React Native Mobile App.
